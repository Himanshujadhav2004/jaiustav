  INDEPENDENT LIVING

  • There are many reasons to move to our independent living home, some of them may be:
  • Loneliness: Seniors may have lost their spouse and their children may not be able to spend sufficient time with them. Loneliness for them can be a major challenge in their daily life. It may also lead to mental illnesses such as depression. At Artha Assisted Living, they are never alone and life for them is fun and engaging.
  • Domestic-help Problems: It is a common problem of finding and managing the right domestic help in our homes. For seniors, it may be an even bigger challenge if they require personal care such as bathing, dressing, etc.

                  Artha Assisted Living > Our Vision

  ASSISTED LIVING

  • As you live through the aging process, the demand for quality care increases. Process of aging makes your seniors require professional help in taking care of them. We ease this process for your loved seniors to live in our thoughtfully designed assisted living homes in India that ensures a comfortable and personalized living experience for seniors.
  • Artha’s assisted living care plan is designed for seniors who may not need constant nursing but require assistance in their activities of daily living (“ADL”). ADL’s include assistance with feeding, bathing, mobility, dressing, continence, and toileting. Artha’s professional care team is trained to assist our residents with their ADLs to provide a hygienic, joyous, and dignified living experience. We also coordinate services by outside health care providers and monitor the resident’s activities to help ensure their safety and well-being.
  • Artha Assisted Living – one of the top luxury senior care homes in India, we are determined to provide a meaningful living experience to seniors in our professionally managed senior friendly home with cleanliness and healthy nutrition.



        POST OPERATIVE CARE

                  Artha Assisted Living > Our Vision

  POST OPERATIVE CARE

  • For seniors, the path to fully recover from an injury or illness is usually very long and stressful. Artha gives you a fully enjoyable and stress-free experience with our holistic senior centric services, specially designed to fit senior lifestyle. We personalize every service and embrace them with the same care and compassion they receive at home to help our residents get back healthy faster.
  • Our team of doctors assess the patient’s situation and medical history to create a care plan that is best suited for full and fast recovery. Our expert team assists our residents in all aspects – from daily routines to scheduled medical examinations.
  • We keep our post-operative recovery residents are under constant medical supervision. Our excellent pain management professionals manage pain through medications as well as massages and hot/cold pad treatment. We have a team of physiotherapists that help manage any mobility issues. The patient’s nutrition also plays an important role in their recovery process and special attention is given to their daily nutritional requirements.
